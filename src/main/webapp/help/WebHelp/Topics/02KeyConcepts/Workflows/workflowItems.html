Workflows based on item collections

More complex workflows can be modelled as a sequence of items or actions.

Each workflow step is an item, and the items have to be completed according to their sequence. This approach fits well when processes are made up of an always similar sequence of workflow steps, like processing a request for proposal, working along check lists, or even an entire flow for processing a customer order.
