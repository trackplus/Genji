My Watch List

This tile gives you a list of items where you are currently registered as either consulted or informed. It permits you to de-register yourself from these items.

Appearance

There is no configuration associated with this tile. It looks like shown below.



You can remove yourself from the watch list by clicking on the „unwatch“ link.
