Reference

The reference basket serves as a place where you collect information that is or might be of some value to you or others. It is important that you organize items here in proper categories to make it easy for you to retrieve them.

Examples for items in the reference basket are frequently asked questions, standards, presentations, contracts, and project specific documentation.
