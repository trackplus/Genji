Flow control

You can define a limit on the number of items that can be assigned to you without an end date and per week. This prevents you from being overwhelmed by too many items piling up in your in-basket.

If somebody wants to place an item into your in-basket, e.g., by delegating something to you, and that would exceed your personal limit, the other person would be informed of the situation and the item would be returned.
