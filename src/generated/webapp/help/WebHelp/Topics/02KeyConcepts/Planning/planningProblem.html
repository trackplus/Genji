The planning process in Track+

To plan projects in Track+ you structure all planable items in a hierarchy and enter planned values for the amount of work, and start and end dates for the schedule.

Expenses in Track+ are planned in terms of working hours. In the most simple case you just enter a planned value for the amount of work estimated to complete an item, and start and end dates. While work progresses, you enter the amount of work spent for each item.

For items having subordinated items (parent items) the dates and expenses are derived from the subordinated items. The earliest start date of all subordinated items is taken as the start date for the parent item, and the latest end date of all subordinated items is taken as the end date for the parent item.
