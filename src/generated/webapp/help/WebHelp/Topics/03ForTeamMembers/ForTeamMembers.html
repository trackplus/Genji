Track+ for team members

Tasks and concepts for team members

This chapter describes procedures for regular project team members, like creating and modifying items, searching for items, or creating reports.

Track+ is widely configurable, and you may find that some screens and terminology on your system is different from what is shown here.

This document cannot explain how Track+ is being used in your organization. In a well run project you will find this information in a project manual and company standards.
