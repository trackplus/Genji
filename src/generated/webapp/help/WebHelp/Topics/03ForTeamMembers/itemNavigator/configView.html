Item navigator / General right area behaviour

Right area configuration

You can configure the right area (views) of the item navigator. What can be configured depends on the view mode.

  1. In the item navigator, click on the "settings" button all the in the top right corner below the logo.


  2. Depending on the view mode you will be able to configure the view.
