Turning off e-mail notifications

If you feel annoyed by the e-mails the Track+ is considered to be one of the most useful.

The e-mails you receive from the Track+ system are marked in a special way, so that you can easily have them sorted automatically by your e-mail client into specific folders. The e-mail subjects look like this:

„[Project yourProject]...“.

Thus you can have your e-mail client sort Track+ e-mails into project specific folders. You can also direct all Track+ messages by the X-Mailer field in the mail header. It is always named „Trackplus“.

  1. In the top toolbar select Administration > My profile > My settings.


  2. Select the tab labeled "Reminder e-mails".


  3. Mark the check box named „Do not send e-mail to me“.
  4. Click on the "Save" button in the tool bar to save your configuration.

You will not get any e-mails from the system any more at all, except for password recovery e-mails.
