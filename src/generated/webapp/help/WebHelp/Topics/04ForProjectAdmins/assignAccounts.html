Assigning accounts

You can plan and monitor work and expenses via accounts that you assign to workspaces. Using accounts you can differentiate between different types of work and expenses, like development, administration, customer requests, and so on.

You must have enabled accounting on the "General" page of the workspace settings.

You must be logged in as space administrator, system manager or system administrator.

  1. In the top toolbar select Administration > Workspaces. Alternatively click on the "Workspaces" bar in the left navigation area.


  2. Select a workspace in the leftmost navigation area.
  3. In the second navigation area click on the node labeled "Assign accounts".


  4. Drag accounts from the rightmost column labeled "Available" to the column "Assigned" to make these accounts available in this workspace. To remove an account drag it back from "Assigned" to "Available".

You can now enter budget and expense information for items in this workspace. When you disable an account, or disable accounting all together for a workspace, existing information will be preserved.
